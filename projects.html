Our projects. What do we do?

Coastal engineering covers a wide variety of working subjects. Our three most recent ones are:

  1. Design of the coastal protection and the main beaches of Costa Rica
  2. Environmental analysis of the impact of fisheries in three main beaches
  3. Description of risk for turists in the Caribbean coast

All these projects intend to improve the quality of life of coastal communities and their users.
